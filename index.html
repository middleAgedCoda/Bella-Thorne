The Origin File

She never
stayed in one lane.

"After Disney, I had the opportunity to find my true self — and that is reflected in all my work from acting, producing, directing, and writing. I have more artistic freedom to continue to express myself."
1997
Born Annabella Avery Thorne · Pembroke Pines, Florida
Italian · Cuban · Irish. Spanish was her first language. Her mother Tamara Thorne is a bestselling horror novelist — the creative DNA is literal. Three siblings all in the industry. At 6 weeks old, she appeared on the cover of Parents Magazine.
2007
Father dies in a traffic accident. Bella is nine years old.
Tamara raises four kids alone. They are broke. "We were living off Stouffer coupons. We were literally about to be on the street if I didn't get that Disney role." Every career move is survival before it is ambition.
–2009
Dyslexia · Bullying · Homeschooled · Self-taught
Diagnosed dyslexic in first grade. Bullied. Homeschooled by her mother. Taught herself to read from cereal boxes and scripts. Taught herself to count from her dad's cash. People told her she couldn't be an actress. "I'm proving them wrong."
2010
Shake It Up · Disney Channel · with Zendaya · 2010–2013
Auditioned to keep the family from being homeless. Three dance classes every night before filming began. Played CeCe Jones — her real dyslexia written into the character. "I wish I had been true to myself." She already knew Disney wasn't the whole story.
2018
Filthy Fangs Records · Epic/Sony · Coachella debut
Launches her own record label. Partners with Epic/Sony. Debuts "B*TCH I'M BELLA THORNE" at Coachella. Releases "GOAT." The artist, not the Disney channel act.
2019
Directs "Her & Him" · Pornhub Vision Award · Oldenburg
Directorial debut — a Shakespearean BDSM short for Pornhub. Screened at the Oldenburg Film Festival. Wins the Pornhub Vision Award on stage. Announces platform algorithm changes in her acceptance speech. A filmmaker from day one.
2019
Posted own nudes after blackmail attempt
A hacker threatened to release her photos. She posted them herself. "They cannot own me if I own myself." This is the thesis of her entire career.
2020
OnlyFans — $1 million in 24 hours · Platform record
First creator in history to earn $1 million in 24 hours on OnlyFans. Crashes the platform. $2M in the first week. $11M+ total. Controversy over the lingerie PPV — OnlyFans changes its payout rules. She generates the controversy, she generates the policy change.
2024
Saint Clare · Taormina · FrightFest · SXSW Sydney · LA Film Awards
Stars in and re-edits Saint Clare. Negotiates for a director's credit. "I know these takes." Wins Best Narrative Feature + Best Director at the LA Independent Women Film Awards. American Psycho meets Joan of Arc. Premiered three festivals in one year.
2025+
Color Your Hurt (director debut, post) · Ash Beneath the Current · Find Your Friends
Color Your Hurt — true story of a gay man abducted in Oklahoma, cast: Christopher Eccleston, Tiffany Haddish. Ash Beneath the Current — shot Tuscany Film Studios. The director is not a side project. She is becoming one of the most interesting filmmakers in the independent space.
The Five Lanes

Five careers.
One person.

Most people pick one thing and do it well. Bella picked five things and did them simultaneously. The chaos is not a weakness — it is the product. No one else has won a Young Artist Award, a Pornhub Vision Award, published a bestselling novel trilogy, set an OnlyFans record, and been accepted into FrightFest in the same career. No one else could.
